This is a tool for saving the alt text you've written on Twitter. If you've downloaded an archive of your tweets you can use the "Archive" functionality and fetch all the alt text you've ever written. Otherwise, you can use the "Recent" functionality to fetch the alt text for your last 3,200 tweets.

This will take some time depending on how many tweets need to be searched, and it's necessary to keep the page open and your computer connected to the internet. Plan on at least two hours, but especially if using an archive it could be many more. The process won't keep your computer or internet connection all that busy, the wait is due to rate limits imposed by Twitter.
